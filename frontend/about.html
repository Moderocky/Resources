Syzygy

is a resource-sharing platform

intended to help creators raise awareness and distribute their open-source projects to other developers and end-users.

Def. syzygy /ˈsɪzɪdʒi/ noun

1. ASTRONOMY

A conjunction or opposition, especially of the moon with the sun. "the planets were aligned in syzygy"

All resources and content is extracted directly from a resource's public GitHub repository

and then supplemented with additional features the author provided.

2. A pair of connected or corresponding things.

Authentication is handled directly by the GitHub API.

Your login data is never seen by Syzygy.

The Syzygy software was written in JavaScript.

Other marketplace-style features are provided such as reviews, comments and discussion to give users more information about the resource they are downloading.

No sensitive user-data is stored by the Syzygy platform.
